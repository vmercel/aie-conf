Conference Registration

  Author Registration

Author registration is compulsory for presentation, publication and proceedings. In order to include the paper in the AIoTSS conference proceedings, one of the authors of each accepted paper must present the paper and complete the registration before the specified deadline. The delegates are also welcome to attend the conference without paper submission provided that an appropriate participant registration fee have been paid.

Special discounts will be made to authors (if requested during registration process). Requests will be entertained on first-come-first-served basis.

Following fee structure is based for virtual conference. In case of physical conference, the amount varies.



Fee: International Participants

Regular Student Participant
Standard $300 $250 $100

* For 'Student Registration', student must be the first author of the paper and need to provide proof of being a student (a valid student certificate/card).

For more than one paper, author need to pay same amount as mentioned above for each paper. Extra pages (more than 6) of submitted publication imply the payment of $30 per page.

Fee: Local Participants

Regular Student Participant
Standard $250 $200 $100

* For 'Student Registration', student must be the first author of the paper and need to provide proof of being a student (a valid student certificate/card).

For more than one paper, author need to pay same amount as mentioned above for each paper. Extra pages (more than 6) of submitted publication imply the payment of $25 per page.
